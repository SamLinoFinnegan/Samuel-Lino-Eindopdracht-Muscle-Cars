The classic Mustang Shelby GT

Songs have been sung and movies have been made about the Mustang Shelby GT, and for good reason: It hits the target with styling, power, and convenience. For the 2017 model year, the Mustang Shelby GT offers a 5.0L V8 that puts out 435 hp, while the 2018-2019 Mustang Shelby GT boasts 460 hp. For the 2019 model year, there's additional differentiation across the GT trim, with the introduction of the GT Premium and the iconic Bullitt model. Across 2017-2019 model years, standard features on the Ford Mustang Shelby GT include:

  • 18-inch wheels
  • Six-speed manual transmission
  • Six-way power cloth front seats with driver-seat power lumbar
  • Dual exhaust with quad tips
  • Highland Green paint option (Bullitt model only)

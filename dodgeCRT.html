The Dodge Charger SRT

As a modern-day muscle car, the Dodge Charger SRT is a great choice if you're seeking something practical and good-looking. It has a slab-sided, retro design that creates a classic-yet-contemporary look. When it comes to power, the 2017 Dodge Charger SRT is decidedly practical, with a standard 292-hp V6 engine. Inside the cabin, there's plenty of room for five. For the 2017 model year (the last with the SRT trim), the Charger boasts an upgraded infotainment system and touchscreen paired with Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ capability. Standard and available features for the 2017 Dodge Charger SE include:

  • 17-inch alloy wheels
  • Power driver seat
  • Keyless entry and ignition
  • Six-speaker sound system
  • Blind-spot monitoring
